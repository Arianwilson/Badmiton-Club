Junior pathway

A clearer route from learning to competitive play

According to the current club site, Unicorn is focused on helping children and young people learn badminton, improve, and move into club and competitive play when ready.

  • Flexible grouping based on age and playing level
  • Parents encouraged to watch or help
  • Safeguarding support and DBS-checked coaching team
  • Volunteer opportunities for older students

Questions and support

Frequently asked questions

These are designed to reduce friction for parents, students and adults who want quick answers before contacting the club.

Who is the club for?

The current club information covers junior development and progression into senior play, making the club suitable for juniors, students and adults depending on session and standard.

Can parents stay during junior sessions?

Yes. The current site explicitly encourages parents to stay and watch or help with activities where appropriate.

What happens with safeguarding concerns?

The existing site provides safeguarding information and a welfare contact route via safeguarding@unicornbadminton.club.
